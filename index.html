Best Engineering Colleges in Mumbai

List of Colleges

  • IIT Bombay

    IIT Bombay is one of the premier engineering institutes in India. Known for its academic excellence and research contributions, the institute offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering and technology. It boasts state-of-the-art facilities and a highly qualified faculty.

  • Thakur College of Engineering and Technology (TCET)

    Thakur College of Engineering and Technology was established in 2001. The college is approved by AICTE, DTE, GOM, and is affiliated with the University of Mumbai. It was ranked 193 by NIRF in 2019.

  • Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute (VJTI)

    Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute (VJTI), founded in 1887, is one of the oldest engineering colleges in Asia. Affiliated with the University of Mumbai, VJTI offers various engineering courses and is known for its rich heritage and academic excellence.

  • >Sardar Patel Institute of Technology

    Sardar Patel Institute of Technology (SPIT), Mumbai was established in 2005 and it is an autonomous unaided research and inauguration institution. SPIT Mumbai is affiliated with the University of Mumbai. SPIT in Mumbai offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral studies in a variety of fields.

  • Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies

    Established in 1981, Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies (NMIMS) is a private deemed-to-be university situated in Mumbai, Maharashtra. Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies offers 2 postgraduate programmes and one postgraduate diploma programme in Management and Business Administration stream

  • Dwarkadas Jivanlal Sanghvi College of Engineering (DJSCOE)

    Dwarkadas Jivanlal Sanghvi College of Engineering (DJSCOE) is an engineering college located in Mumbai. Established in 1994, it offers various engineering programs and is known for its strong industry connections.

  • Vivekanand Education Society's Institute of Technology (VESIT)

    VESIT is an engineering institute affiliated with the University of Mumbai. Established in 1984, it offers a range of engineering programs and is known for its strong emphasis on quality education.

  • Thadomal Shahani Engineering College

    Thadomal Shahani Engineering College (TSEC) is an engineering college located in Mumbai. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programs and is known for its academic excellence and industry-oriented approach.

  • KJ Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology (KJSIEIT)

    KJ Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology (KJSIEIT) is affiliated with the University of Mumbai. It offers a variety of engineering programs and is known for its quality education and innovative approach to learning.

  • Vidyalankar Institute of Technology

    Vidyalankar Institute of Technology (VIT) is an engineering college located in Mumbai. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programs and is known for its student-centric approach and academic excellence.
